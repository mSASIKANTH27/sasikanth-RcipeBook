White Castle Pizzas

Preparation Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 35 mins

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Mushrooms, sliced
  • Green bell pepper, diced
  • Onions, sliced
  • Olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough and place it on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough and add mozzarella cheese.
  4. Top with pepperoni, mushrooms, green bell pepper, and onions.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, slice, and enjoy your White Castle Pizzas!
