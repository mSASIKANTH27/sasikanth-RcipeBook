Kung Pao Chicken BBQ

Preparation Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 25 mins

Total Time: 45 mins

Ingredients:

  • Chicken thighs, boneless and skinless
  • Soy sauce
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sesame oil
  • Honey
  • Garlic, minced
  • Ginger, grated
  • Red chili flakes
  • Peanuts, roasted
  • Green onions, sliced
  • Cooked white rice, for serving

Directions:

  1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, honey, garlic, ginger, and red chili flakes to make the marinade.
  2. Cut chicken thighs into bite-sized pieces and marinate in the sauce for at least 15 minutes.
  3. Heat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Thread marinated chicken pieces onto skewers and grill until cooked through.
  5. Brush with additional marinade during grilling if desired.
  6. Serve Kung Pao Chicken BBQ over cooked white rice, garnished with roasted peanuts and sliced green onions.
  7. Enjoy your Kung Pao Chicken BBQ!
